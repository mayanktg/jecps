The Golden Foundation

Jabalpur Engineering College (estd. 1947) is one of the oldest technical institutions in the country and one of the first colleges to have had its very own Photographic Society.

The Photographic Society of Jabalpur Engineering College was formed in the year 1959 , when cameras were a rare possession and the art of photography was cumbersome and expensive;but appreciated nevertheless.

Early History

It was initiated with Mr. RN Choudhari as the president.

It witnessed the growth of the film and plate formats through the 60s and the advent of oscilloscopes and TLR cameras in the 70s.

A New Re-Begining

As of 2012, there has been a tremendous advancement in the field of photography and photo-editing : and every amateur loves to experiment with the D-SLR he owns.

Thus, the JEC Photographic Society has been revived in this period full of possibilities and opportunities for exploring this evergreen hobby!
