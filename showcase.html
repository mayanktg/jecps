Are you ready?

Photography, a beautiful talent to possess. In today's world a person clicks a thousand pictures and yet for him, it is just a captured moment.
So, when do you explore your photograhic talent?

Lets see a scenario

One day you are walking down the street, suddenly you see children playing football in the mud.
You become overwhelmed and decide to capture this moment. You realize that you are always carrying a camera attached to your phone. You take it out, and on your own you click it, at the right moment, at the right angle and of course standing at the right place.
And then afterwards when you see the image on a bigger screen, only one word comes into the mind "unbelievable"! You smile looking at them and remember those moments.

Sharing...A good Idea

Every picture has a story behind it which you love to share with your family, friends and the special someone. Then you think, why not post this on Flickr, facebook or Instagram. And within no time you are waiting for the likes and comments! But then only one that makes your click worth is always something like
"Wow! You can be a photographer."

So What you decide now ??

We should always do things we love and know its right rather than what we are forced to do! Does it really matters if we wear socks in the wrong legs and eat with the left hand?
So if you are really interested in chosing Photography as your passion, go ahead and ping us. We would be happy to help in every possible way.

Still confused? ..huhh

Still confused over the things you love to do. Take a leave, look around your world. Go in the park, sit alone, observe the nature, watch kids playing, the dawning Sun..... If your mind tells you to take out your phone and capture the moment , here it goes
--Welcome to the world of Photographers
